Restricted & Protected Data

The following information is intentionally protected by Roblox's security and privacy systems. This application does not attempt to access or bypass these safeguards.

  • Email address
  • Password or authentication data
  • IP address or precise geographic location
  • Private inventory (when visibility is restricted)
  • Direct messages and chat logs
  • Robux balance and transaction history
  • Trade history and inbound/outbound trades

Certain features such as joining a user’s game or viewing their activity may also be limited depending on the user's privacy settings, server type, or platform restrictions.
